About Simmra Aveera

Simmra Aveera Private Limited was Incorporated in February 2026 in Bengaluru, India, with a Long-Term Goal to Build Entities that Resolve Complex Challenges through Responsible and Modern Development.

Established during a Transformative Era where Artificial Intelligence and Technology were fundamentally reshaping Global Economies, Our Firm was founded on the Conviction that such Advancements must be harnessed to deliver Scalable and Sustainable Outcomes for Businesses, The EdTech Landscape, and the Communities we serve.

The Meaning Behind Our Name

SIMMRA

Inspired by the name of our Managing Director's Daughter, Simmran. It represents Reflection, Remembrance, Gratitude and a Conscious Connection to Higher Values.

AVEERA

Symbolizes Courage, Resilience and the Unwavering Determination to Persevere despite Adversity.

Together, Simmra Aveera represents the Union of Purpose and Fortitude that Guides Every Decision, Initiative and Relationship We Create.

Built on Experience

Founded by Managing Director Ruhhi Siingh, a Seasoned Professional with more than Two Decades of Expertise, Our Venture is Engineered on the belief that Enduring Organisations Identify Real-World Challenges and Resolve them through Strategic Planning, Disciplined Execution and Ethical Leadership.

As an Udyam Registered Enterprise headquartered in Bengaluru, we serve as the Parent Company for Independent Business Verticals, each operating with a distinct Identity while remaining unified by our Commitment to Integrity, Precision and Long-Term Value Creation.
