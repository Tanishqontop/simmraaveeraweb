About Us

Simmra Aveera Private Limited was Incorporated in February 2026 in Bengaluru, India, with a Long-Term Goal to Build Entities that Resolve Complex Challenges through Responsible and Modern Development. Established during a Transformative Era where Artificial Intelligence and Technology were fundamentally reshaping Global Economies, Our Firm was founded on the Conviction that such Advancements must be harnessed to deliver Scalable and Sustainable Outcomes for Businesses, The EdTech Landscape, and the Communities we serve.

The Name Simmra Aveera

The name Simmra Aveera reflects the Foundational Principles of our work:

‘Simmra’

Inspired by the name of our Managing Director’s Daughter, Simmran. In our tradition, the name signifies Reflection, Remembrance, and a Conscious Connection to High Values. And for Ruhhi, it represents Purpose, Gratitude, and the enduring Inspiration of her Motherhood.

‘Aveera’

Symbolizes Resilience, Courage, and the Unwavering Determination to Persevere despite Adversity.

Together, “Simmra Aveera” represents the Union of Purpose and Fortitude that Guides Every Decision, Initiative, and Relationship we Create.

Founded by Managing Director Ruhhi Siingh, a Seasoned Professional with more than Two Decades of Expertise, Our Venture is Engineered on the belief that Enduring Organisations Identify Real-World Challenges and Resolve them through Strategic Planning, Disciplined Execution, and Ethical Leadership. As an Udyam-Registered Enterprise headquartered in Bengaluru, we serve as the Parent Company for Independent Business Verticals, each operating with a distinct Identity while remaining unified by our Commitment to Integrity, Precision, and Long-Term Value Creation.

‘A Mother-Daughter Alliance’

Our Divisions

Today, the Group operates through Two Primary Divisions:

  1. Simra Ruhhi Strategy & Setu

    Our Executive Consulting and Advisory Unit, Partnering with Founders and Institutions to strengthen Leadership, Operational Capability, Digital Excellence, and Consistent Growth.

  2. Nimma Setu

    Our Flagship EdTech Initiative, conceptualized to strengthen Academic Ecosystems across South India. Built on a Student-First-Culture, Nimma Setu delivers Integrated Programs for NEET, IIT-JEE, KCET, and Foundation Competitive Examinations. By combining Scholarly Excellence, Structured Mentorship, and Technology-Enabled Learning, we provide a Disciplined Approach to Measurable Outcomes. Designed in Bengaluru for Aspirants across the Region, this Endeavour reflects Our Commitment to transforming Education.
